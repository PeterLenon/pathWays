# PRD: AI Income Path Optimizer ## Project Overview An AI-powered system designed to help individuals in Mississippi (and beyond) identify the most effective pathways to increase their earning potential. By analyzing personal data and local labor market conditions, the tool provides data-driven, personalized career recommendations, balancing cost, risk, and long-term income growth. ## Target Audience - High school students and recent graduates in Mississippi. - Individuals in low-income brackets seeking career transitions. - Displaced workers or those looking to upskill. ## Key Goals 1. **Reduce Uncertainty:** Provide clear, localized data on career outcomes. 2. **Expand Horizons:** Evaluate non-traditional paths (trade schools, certifications) alongside college degrees. 3. **Mitigate Risk:** Explicitly address financial constraints and educational barriers. 4. **Empower Mobility:** Drive better long-term health outcomes through increased economic stability. ## Core Features & Functionality ### 1. Personalized Assessment (Intake) - Simple, supportive onboarding to capture: - Current education/skills. - Financial constraints (budget for education, immediate income needs). - Geographic preferences (specifically focusing on Mississippi/Hinds County). - Career interests and values. ### 2. Multi-Pathway Optimizer (AI Core) - Evaluates: College Degrees, Trade Schools, Certifications, Direct Workforce Entry. - Predicts: Potential income (salary ranges), time to completion, and ROI. - Factors in: Local job demand (LinkedIn/Indeed data), tuition costs (local MS institutions). ### 3. Comparison & Discovery Tool - Side-by-side comparison of different paths (e.g., "Nursing Degree" vs. "HVAC Certification"). - Visualizes "Income vs. Cost vs. Time." ### 4. Guided Next Steps (Action Plan) - Personalized roadmap for the chosen path. - Integration with local resources (financial aid info, application links, mentorship programs). ## Proposed Screens for Design 1. **Welcome / Mission Screen:** Sets the tone, explains the connection between income and health, and starts the journey. 2. **Personalized Intake (Step-based):** A clean, approachable form to gather user data without being overwhelming. 3. **Pathways Dashboard (The Optimizer):** The primary view showing the AI's top recommendations with key stats (salary, cost, demand). 4. **Path Detail / Comparison View:** Deep dive into a specific career path with a roadmap and detailed "Why this works for you" AI insights. ## Design Aesthetic - **Trustworthy & Professional:** High-quality typography, clean layout. - **Empowering & Optimistic:** Vibrant but grounded color palette (e.g., deep blues, supportive greens). - **Accessible:** Clear hierarchy, readable text, and mobile-responsive layout (crucial for this demographic).
